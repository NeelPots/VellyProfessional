About Us

Get to know about our company

Velly Professional Services Ltd (VPS) Accountants was established since 2012 to provide all the accountancy requirements to any small to medium sized business as per their requirement. We are an accountancy practice who looks after small to medium businesses. Our service is tailored to your needs we at VPS will work as part of your team, providing a level of service and professional qualification equal to larger accountancy firms but at an affordable price.

Our team is always there to help you, with many years of experience, working in industry, payroll bureau, and in large firm of accountancy, enabling us to offer a broad range of knowledge and advice, leaving you more time and peace of mind to run your business.

Our clients’ long-term sustainable development and growth is our top priority. We provide a comprehensive and flexible range of services to our clients specialising in accountancy, Bookkeeping, Payroll, self-assessment and taxation services. We serve a wide spectrum of clients, ranging from small to medium size limited companies to private individuals.
